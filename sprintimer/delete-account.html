DELETE YOUR ACCOUNT

Last updated: May 1, 2026

You can delete your Sprintimer account and all associated data directly from within the app, or by submitting a request to us.

Delete From The App (Recommended)

  1. Open Sprintimer
  2. Tap Account in the bottom navigation
  3. Tap Log Out to sign out first if needed
  4. Your data deletion request can be submitted by emailing us at virtuostudios@gmail.com with the subject line "Delete My Account" and your registered email address

What Gets Deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • All sprint records and personal bests
  • Your profile data (name, height, weight, date of birth)
  • All synced data from your devices

Retention Period

Some data may be retained for up to 30 days in backups before being permanently deleted.

Contact Us

Email: virtuostudios@gmail.com

We will process your request within 7 days.
